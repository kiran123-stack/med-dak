Why is preventive maintenance critical for Indian hospitals?

Given the fluctuating power grids, humidity variations, and high patient loads typical across Indian facilities, regular servicing prevents catastrophic failure, extends equipment life by up to 40%, and ensures near-100% diagnostic accuracy. Preventive care isn't a cost centre — it's how you protect the capital already sitting on your floor.

How does Med DAK guarantee on-time service?

We operate a localized hub-and-spoke model across 14 cities. Certified biomedical engineers and critical spares stay within a few hours of every contracted facility. Emergency dispatch runs 24/7, and every AMC / CMC contract carries a written response SLA.

What equipment categories does Med DAK service?

Imaging (CT, MRI, X-ray, ultrasound), patient monitoring, ventilators and anaesthesia machines, ICU infusion & syringe pumps, dialysis units, sterilizers, and lab analyzers. We service multi-brand fleets — you don't need separate contracts per OEM.

What's the difference between AMC and CMC in real terms?

An AMC (Annual Maintenance Contract) covers scheduled preventive care and labor — spare parts are billed separately. A CMC (Comprehensive Maintenance Contract) is all-inclusive: parts, labor, calibration, and emergency response are already priced into a single annual fee. CMC gives you a predictable budget and zero-surprise operations.

Do you provide NABH and NABL compliance documentation?

Yes. Every calibration, preventive check-up, and repair generates traceable, audit-ready records aligned with NABH and NABL requirements. Documentation is delivered digitally and archived so accreditation cycles stop being a scramble.

Can we start with a single audit before committing to a contract?

Absolutely — that's often the smartest first step. Book a maintenance audit and we'll walk your biomedical inventory, benchmark condition, and give you a written recommendation. No obligation to move to a contract.
